Common Questions

Is this therapy or medical advice?

No. This is educational content about Complex PTSD and trauma recovery. It's designed to complement therapy, not replace it. Always consult with licensed healthcare providers for medical advice.

How much time do I need each week?

The self-paced bundle requires 2-3 hours weekly. The mentorship includes weekly 90-minute group calls plus community interaction. Both are designed for busy adults.

What if I can't afford either option?

We offer scholarships for those with financial need. Email daniel@groundedcptsd.com to inquire. The free curriculum PDF is always available to everyone.
